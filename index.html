About Me

I bring over a decade of hands-on experience in MEP drafting and engineering, with a strong background in fire sprinkler design and familiarity with mechanical design. My experience includes developing detailed fire protection and mechanical drawings, peforming and cross-checking hydraulic and load calculations, visiting sites to survey, and ensuring that each part complies with NYC Building Codes and their respective code standards.


I am fully commited to the philosphy of life-long learning. Researching and understanding local codes, regulations, and MEP code standards is a general practice of mine. When I don't have my engineer's cap I like to spend my time reading, visiting a park, and spending time with family and community.
